Powerful Features for Eye Comfort

SuperDimmer goes beyond simple screen dimming with intelligent detection and targeted dimming that other apps can't match.

Intelligent Detection

Automatically detects bright areas on your screen using advanced luminance analysis. Only dims what needs dimming.

Per-Region Dimming

Our killer feature: dim specific bright areas within windows. Perfect for dark mode apps with bright content.

Color Temperature

Built-in f.lux-style blue light filter. Reduce blue light at night with warm color temperature presets.

Active/Inactive Windows

Apply different dim levels to foreground and background windows. Focus on what matters.
