Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase if you are not satisfied with SuperDimmer Pro.

1. Eligibility for Refund

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • Your purchase was made within the last 30 days
  • You are not satisfied with the Software for any reason
  • You have not violated our Terms of Service

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us at support@superdimmer.app with your purchase receipt or license key
  2. Include a brief explanation of why you're requesting a refund (optional but helpful)
  3. We will process your refund within 5-7 business days

Alternatively, you can request a refund directly through Paddle if you purchased through them.

3. Refund Processing

3.1 Processing Time

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days of approval. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for purchase.

3.2 Refund Amount

You will receive a full refund of the purchase price. No fees are deducted.

4. After Refund

Upon refund approval:

  • Your Pro license will be deactivated
  • You may continue using the Free tier of SuperDimmer
  • You are welcome to repurchase Pro at any time

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refund:

  • Purchases made more than 30 days ago
  • Refunds requested due to violation of Terms of Service
  • Free tier features (no payment was made)

6. Disputes

If you have any concerns about your refund request, please contact us at support@superdimmer.app. We're committed to resolving any issues fairly and promptly.

7. Contact

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please contact:

Email: support@superdimmer.app
